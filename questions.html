IKIGAI

Ikigai is something you can do every day for as little as 10 minutes that gives intense Satisfaction and pleasure, this acute pleasure is enough to make you immune to stress and Loneliness for the whole day.

Ikigai is not a hobby or passion, it's the purpose of your Life. This has the potential to even turn into your career and earn a living while bringing positive change to society.

To find your IKigai or Purpose of your life Answer the following Questions .

What is it that you love ?

To truly understand and find what you love, consider the following underlying questions,only then this broad sense of “ Love” will be of meaning.

What would you do if money was never the option ?

Ex-Photography, Drawing, Travel..

On what Topic can you talk at length to a room full of audience ?

Ex -Using Mobile to take amazing Photographs, Travel with practically no money, Solving Problems.

When working on something-You enjoy the task without worrying and you lose track of time?

Ex- Drawing a scene , Searching for the correct angle to take a picture, Designing Products..

What are you good at ?

To truly understand and find what you are really good at , consider the following underlying questions,only then this broad sense of “ Good” will be of meaning.

What practical skills are you talented at ?

Ex- Photography and Drawing.

Have you won any awards - If yes, what skills did you use to achieve it ?

Ex- Drawing

What are the skills that you feel easy to do, while others might not ?

Ex- Brainstorming and finding solutions.

What does the world need from you ?

Are there any social/environmental or economic problems that you want to help solve?

Ex - Emotional development, Employment etc

Are there any issues in your community or work that touch you emotionally?

Ex - Lack of Help for those in need of emotional support.

If you were to ask your family/close friends around you, What would they say that you support for ?

Ex - Empathy

What can you get paid for?

What Skills are you getting paid for? ( or what skills will you be using to get paid for in near future)

Ex - Photography, Engineering Degree

Do you use your talents and passion in what you do for a career?

Ex - No

How can you use the skills you are good at and you love doing to earn money ?

Ex- Freelancing photography
