Who is a Peer Counselor?

Peer Counselors are not professional therapists or Psychologists , but they are trained peers or friends who have an extensive Psychology background like M.A or Msc clinical Psychology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is peer Counseling completely free ?

Yes, There are no charges.

Is Peer Counseling effective ?

It is highly subjective to your case, take our free mental wellness test to know more about the cause, severity and treatment about your case. Take Test

Why should I mention my WhatsApp number ?

You can connect with a peer Counselor through whatsapp and proceed further via phone.

Does findhope record/use any of my personal information ?

No we do not record/save any of the users personal issue but we end the Counselling session with a feedback , this is the only information we save.

Does findhope have Psychologists ?

Yes, we work with Psychologists.Infact, a peer Counselor may recommend professional intervention depending on a user's case.

How can I be a peer Counselor ?

Anyone can be a Peer Counselors given that they are passionate about Counselling , while not expecting a fee and can go through a training session. Get Started
